About Institute

Sri Sai Private Industrial Training Institute is NCVT affiliated ITI conducting Industrial training and education in the areas of Electrician, Welder, Diesel Mechanic and Fitter. It was established in the year 1984 with a mission to provide technical education mainly to the rural youth. The Sri Sai Private ITI is situated at Pendurthi, Visakhapatnam in the state of Andhra Pradesh.

Sri Sai Private Industrial Training Institute run by Sri Sai Educational Society has a legacy of thirty five years of service to the cause of education established in 1984. It is an Institution with distinctive academic profile, blending itself in commitment and preparing the rural youth for the modern challenges of today and empowering them by training with job oriented trades.

Founder Message

I am delighted to welcome you to Sri Sai Private ITI, a renowed centre for industrial training centre in this city of Visakhapatnam. We started our journey in the year of 1984 by Sri Sai Educational Society under the leadership of late Sri S Apparao garu in a eco friendly atmosphere to fulfill the motto of quality technical education with a idea of mainstreaming of rural youth by providing them access to productive & decent work through skill development route by providing de-novo skilling to fresher, up-skilling to ITI graduates, re-skilling to existing workers.

Sri S Apparao

Sri S Gopala Krishna

Under the able leadership of Sri S Gopala Krishna, Correspondent the ITI has achieved new heights and has stepped into various areas and has been pioneering in all phases. The ITI has been serving in the trades of Fitter, Electrician, Welder and Diesel Mechanic. ITI Trainees can work in various specialized area like railway, automotive system, various industry sectors, design engineering, dynamic system and control, power plant engineering, etc.

The institute has well qualified and experienced faculty members and they are committed to excellence in teaching. Interactive relationship is maintained between students and the staff which enables the students to develop sound foundation. The faculty aims at putting extra ordinary effort towards motivating the students in solving actual industrial problems by undertaking various projects, industrial visits and in-plant training. The Institution is actively associated in grooming the trainees in ITI by nurturing them with inputs from various basic and specialized subjects. You are all invited to join this institution to find your technical future and become well skilled trainees in the recent future
